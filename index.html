Awards

Adding Apple Watch Fitness awards to your home screen made easy

Public beta testing available here.

Info

Awards is a lightweight app that allows users to manually add their Fitness achievements and display them on the iPhone's home screen.

Features

Responsive

The app is natively made for iPhone, so the responsiveness is never an issue.

Customizable

All awards are added manually, so you control which ones show up on your home screen!

Privacy

There is no data leaving your phone, making your privacy our priority.

Securely store inside a databse

All awards are store inside an external database, so they become available seemingly, as soon as possible.
